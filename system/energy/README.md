### ğŸ”‹ Energy System

Energy is a **core resource** required to obtain hunting rewards in EXTOCIUM.

Once both **Free Energy** and **Paid Energy** are fully consumed,\
**items and gold will no longer drop.**

The Energy System exists to prevent excessive item supply in the game\
and to maintain the value of items obtained through hunting.

### â—¾ Free Energy Consumption

While playing a Hero,\
Energy decreases by **1** each time a certain amount of EXP is gained, based on the Heroâ€™s level.

When hunting monsters that are **11 or more levels lower** than your Hero,\
Energy consumption is calculated based on the **monsterâ€™s level**,\
not your Heroâ€™s level.

For example,\
if your Hero is level 21 and you hunt a level 10 monster,\
the **Level Cap System** applies, and Energy consumption follows the **level 10 Energy table**.

When hunting monsters **higher than your Heroâ€™s level**,\
more EXP is gained, so Energy may feel like it is consumed **much faster**.

### â—¾ Paid Energy Purchase

If you wish to continue hunting,\
you can purchase additional Energy from the [shop](../../economy/shop/) using **BNB or Gems**.

* Each Hero can charge up to **2,000 Paid Energy per week**.
