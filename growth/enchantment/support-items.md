#### 1ï¸âƒ£ Rune of Fortune

**Rune of Fortune** is an item that **increases the Enchantment success rate** of equipment.

* **How to Obtain**
  * Can be crafted through the [**Crafting system**](../../beginners-guide/gameplay-guide/crafting.md).

* **Additional Info**
  * **Rune of Fortune Lv.4** can only be obtained through [**Lucky Boxes**](../../economy/loot-box-info/lucky-box.md).

| Rune of Fortune lv.4 | 40                           |
