### âš”ï¸ Weapon Skill Types

Weapon skills are divided into **five main types**.\
Each type differs in attack method and tactical purpose,\
allowing you to choose the right skill for each combat situation.

***

#### 1. AoE (Area of Effect)

Attack skills that deal damage over a **wide area**.

* Can hit multiple enemies at once
* Highly effective against clustered enemies
* Ideal for area-focused combat situations

***

#### 2. Projectile

Skills that attack distant enemies by **launching projectiles**.

* Can strike enemies at long range
* **Range and projectile speed** are key factors
* Specialized for long-range combat

***

#### 3. Summon

Skills that **summon units** to assist in battle.

* Summoned units automatically attack enemies
* Can serve offensive or supportive roles
* Effective for applying constant pressure over time

***

#### 4. Buff&#x20;

Skills that apply **enhancement effects** to the user.

* Increase attributes such as attack power, defense, and movement speed
* Improve overall combat efficiency and survivability
* Most effective when combined with other skills

***

#### 5. Melee

Skills that attack enemies at **close range**.

* Deal powerful damage within a short range
* Characterized by fast strikes and high damage output
* Specialized for close-quarters combat

***
