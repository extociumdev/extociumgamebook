### 🍓 X Points Earning Rate

The XTO Holding System has been upgraded!

Now, when you hold XTO, you automatically earn **X Points**.\
Your earned X Points can be exchanged for various items inside the game.

***

#### ◾ How to Earn X Points

1️⃣ Purchase an XTO Ticket.\
2️⃣ Hold your ticket.\
3️⃣ Receive X Points automatically every week.

There is now **no holding cap** on XTO. The more you hold, the more you earn.\
Hold as much as you want and grow your points freely!

{% hint style="info" %}
#### 💡 **GEM / XTO / BNB** **Usage Guide**

X Points are awarded 👉 based on the amount spent in the in-game SHOP.\
No matter which currency you use — GEM, XTO or BNB —\
points are calculated **according to your SHOP purchase amount**.

Please keep this in mind when earning or using your points.
{% endhint %}
