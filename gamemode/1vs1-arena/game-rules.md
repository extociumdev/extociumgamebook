To enter the 1vs1 Arena, the following conditions must be met:

### â—¾ Basic Rules

* **Match Duration**: 2 minutes

#### Victory Conditions

* Defeat your opponent to win immediately.
* If neither player is defeated within 2 minutes,\
  the player with the **higher Damage Score** wins.

#### Damage Score

* Damage Score is accumulated\
  **each time you deal damage to your opponent** after the match begins.

#### Map Selection

* Once matchmaking is complete, **1 out of 4 Arena maps** is selected at random.

***

* **Food Buffs**: Up to **1** can be used
* **Potion Limits**
  * HP Potions: 5
  * Companion HP Potions: 5
  * MP Potions: 20 _(Potion grade does not affect these limits)_
* **MP Cost Reduction**
  * Skill MP consumption is reduced by **20%** in the Arena.

  * If no players are available in the same League,\
    matchmaking expands to **one League above and below**.

#### Level Matching Criteria

* Matchable level range: **Â±10 levels**
* The matching level is based on **the highest-level Hero owned on your account**
* This rule prevents abuse by repeatedly using low-level Heroes.

#### Consecutive Match Restriction

* The opponent from your **previous match** \
  will be excluded from your next matchmaking pool.

***

### â—¾ Bot Matchmaking

If no suitable opponent is found, a **bot match** will be initiated automatically.

* Bots have **higher HP and Attack Power** than standard players.
